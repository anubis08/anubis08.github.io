About ElderTech


ElderTech is a youth-led organization dedicated to enhancing technological literacy among seniors, helping them stay engaged, independent, and digitally confident.

Our Mission


We aim to bridge the digital divide for seniors by offering interactive workshops, personalized guidance, and hands-on technology education.

Why We Exist


Many seniors face technological barriers that limit their communication, access to information, and daily independence. ElderTech empowers them to thrive in a digital world.

Where We Operate


We host workshops and offer resources to seniors in communities all over Los Angeles, Dallas, and Atlanta.

How You Can Help


Join us as a volunteer, donate resources, or spread awareness. Your support helps us make technology accessible and enjoyable for seniors.
