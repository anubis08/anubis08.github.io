ElderTech Engaged

Empowering seniors through technology education workshops engagement

A youth-led organization dedicated to enhancing technological literacy among seniors, empowering them to navigate the digital world with confidence. By providing tailored workshops and one-on-one coaching, we help seniors stay connected with loved ones and access essential services. Additionally, our focus on technology education keeps their brains active, contributing to the mitigation of dementia and other cognitive diseases. Our mission is to foster independence and improve the quality of life for older adults through the power of technology.
